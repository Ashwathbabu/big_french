Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers. No pressure. No surprises.

Everything you need to know before starting — already thought through.

Is this suitable even if I'm starting from zero?
Absolutely! Our curriculum is designed to take you from complete beginner (A0) to advanced levels. We start with the basics and build up progressively.
In which languages are lessons explained?
Lessons are available in English, Hindi, and Punjabi, so complex concepts are easy to understand.
How long does it take to become exam-ready?
Most students reach exam readiness in 3-6 months with consistent study, but it varies based on your starting level and study intensity.
Can I study using my phone?
Yes! Our platform is fully mobile-responsive, allowing you to study on your phone, tablet, or computer.
Is this course for TEF Canada or TCF Canada?
We offer specialized tracks for both exams. You can choose your target exam at the beginning, and all materials will be tailored to that specific test format.
Will I actually get feedback on speaking and writing?
Yes! Our AI-powered evaluation provides instant, detailed feedback on your speaking and writing responses, highlighting areas for improvement.
Are the mock tests close to the real exam?
Our mock tests are designed to mirror the exact format, timing, and difficulty level of the actual TEF and TCF exams.
Do I need to buy extra books or materials?
No, everything you need is included in the course. However, we may recommend supplementary resources for extra practice if desired.
How do I know the guidance is reliable?
Our curriculum is designed by certified instructors with years of experience and proven student success rates.
What if I miss classes or fall behind?
All lessons are recorded and available on-demand, so you can learn at your own pace and catch up whenever needed.
Will I know exactly where I stand?
Yes, our detailed progress tracking and performance reports show you exactly where you stand in each module.
What if my score doesn't improve as fast as I expect?
Our AI analysis identifies your weak areas so you can focus your efforts. Plus, our support team is always available to help adjust your study plan.
What happens when my plan ends?
You can easily renew or upgrade your plan. Your progress and data are saved so you can continue seamlessly.
What if I'm not good at languages?
Our methodical, step-by-step approach breaks down complex concepts into manageable pieces. Many of our successful students thought the same initially!
I've tried courses before. How is this different?
We focus specifically on TEF/TCF exam success with AI feedback, structured curriculum, and real exam simulations — not generic French lessons.
Will this feel overwhelming?
Our bite-sized lessons and clear progression path make learning manageable. You set the pace that works for you.
What if I lose motivation halfway?
Our community support, progress tracking, and regular milestones help keep you motivated throughout your journey.
