Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long do I have access to the TEF course?
You have access for 6 months from the date of purchase. This gives you plenty of time to complete all lessons, take mock tests, and review materials before your exam.
Is this course enough to get CLB 9+?
Yes! Our comprehensive curriculum and AI-powered practice tests are designed to help you achieve CLB 9 or higher. 94% of our students who complete the course achieve their target scores.
Can I switch between TEF and TCF later?
Yes, you can switch between courses within 7 days of purchase. Both exams test similar skills, so your progress will transfer over.
What if I'm a complete beginner?
Our course starts from A1 level and takes you to B2/C1. We cover all grammar, vocabulary, and exam strategies you need, regardless of your starting point.
How are the mock tests evaluated?
Our AI evaluates your speaking and writing instantly. For listening and reading, you get immediate results with detailed explanations for each answer.
How long is the course valid?
Course access is valid for 6 months. Mock test plans vary from 1-6 months depending on your selected plan.
In which languages are lessons explained?
Lessons are explained in English, with options for Hindi and Punjabi explanations for complex grammar concepts.
Can I access the course on mobile?
Yes! Our platform is fully responsive. You can study on your phone, tablet, or computer — anywhere, anytime.
